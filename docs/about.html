About Us

Learn about our journey, mission, and the team behind Circle Code Shipping

Our Story

Circle Code Shipping was founded in 2015 with a clear vision: to revolutionize the shipping industry by providing online sellers with reliable, cost-effective shipping solutions.

What started as a small operation with just three employees has now grown into a nationwide network of logistics professionals serving thousands of sellers across the country. Our growth has been fueled by our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction and technological innovation.

Today, we're proud to be one of the fastest-growing shipping solutions providers in the market, trusted by businesses of all sizes from small Etsy shops to large e-commerce platforms.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, efficient, and affordable shipping solutions that help sellers grow their businesses and deliver exceptional experiences to their customers.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted shipping partner for online sellers worldwide, setting new standards for reliability, speed, and customer service in the logistics industry.

Our Values

Reliability, transparency, innovation, environmental responsibility, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction guide everything we do at Circle Code Shipping.
